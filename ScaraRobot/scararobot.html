About


The term SCARA is an acronym that stands for Selective Compliant Assembly Robot Arm. For packaging processes that require repetitive, manually intensive product handling, robotics is the ideal solution. Bholanath presents 4 axis SCARA robots for pick and place applications to automate high volume manufacturing lines. These robotic systems enable greater through puts, better quality and more reliabiltiy. Bholanath SCARA robots are well suited for packaging and material handling applications.


Bholanath introduces 4 models from 500 gms to 5 kg payloads, cycle time as low as 2.5 seconds, pick and place with an accuracy of +/- 0.05mm and life cycle of 20,000 hrs.
